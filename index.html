absi.cc

absi.cc is an online platform that raises awareness about the rights of persons with disabilities in Libya and around the world. The blog covers a variety of topics related to disability, including the challenges faced by persons with disabilities in Libya, the importance of education and employment for persons with disabilities, and the need to raise awareness about disability issues.

The blog is managed by Abdusalam Mustafa Shlebak, a Libyan blogger who is passionate about the rights of persons with disabilities. Shlebak has been blogging since 2009, and has published over 100 articles about the rights of persons with disabilities.

absi.cc aims to make a difference in the lives of persons with disabilities in Libya. The site seeks to raise awareness about disability issues, promote the rights of persons with disabilities, and support persons with disabilities in achieving their goals.

absi.cc is an important platform for persons with disabilities in Libya. The blog provides a space for persons with disabilities to express their experiences, share information, and support each other. The site is also an important resource for people who want to learn more about the rights of persons with disabilities in Libya.
